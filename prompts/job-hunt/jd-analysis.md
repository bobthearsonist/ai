# Job Posting Summary Prompt

Create a comprehensive job posting summary using my resume as reference. Analyze the job requirements and structure the summary as follows:

## Format

### Quick Reference Table

Create a table with three columns: "Job Requirement" | "Status" | "Your Experience & Examples"

**Status indicators:**
- ✅ **Exceeds** - Experience significantly beyond requirement
- ✅ **Excellent** / **Strong** / **Proven** - Direct match with strong examples
- ✅ **Relevant** / **Yes** - Transferable or applicable experience
- 🟡 **Transferable** - Indirect but relevant experience
- ⚠️ **Gap** - Limited or no direct experience (be honest)

**Organize requirements into sections:**

1. **REQUIRED SKILLS** - Technical requirements and tools
2. **RESPONSIBILITIES** - Key job duties
3. **COMPANY FIT** - Location, domain knowledge, work style

For each requirement, provide specific examples from my resume including:
- Company names where I used these skills
- Scale/impact metrics (e.g., "70M users", "$762B annually")
- Specific technologies or frameworks
- Leadership roles if applicable

### Interview Talking Points

Organize into 3-4 subsections:

**Technical Depth**
- Key technical alignments between job requirements and my experience
- Specific examples of similar work or technologies
- Scale and complexity indicators

**Leadership Value** (if applicable)
- Team leadership experience
- Mentoring and coaching
- Process improvements

**Unique Advantages**
- Numbered list (3-5 items) of standout qualifications
- What makes me particularly strong for this role
- Combinations of skills that are rare or valuable

**Areas to Discuss** (optional, if gaps exist)
- Honest assessment of skills to develop
- How transferable experience applies
- Enthusiasm for learning specific technologies

## Analysis Guidelines

1. **Be specific and quantitative** - Include metrics, user counts, dollar amounts, team sizes
2. **Map technical skills precisely** - Don't assume equivalence; note both matches and gaps
3. **Highlight leadership** - Tech lead, Scrum Master, mentoring experience is valuable
4. **Show scale** - Enterprise systems, high-traffic platforms, complex architectures
5. **Be honest about gaps** - Better to address honestly than overstate
6. **Focus on transferable skills** - Event-driven architecture, distributed systems, cloud-native patterns
7. **Calculate overall match percentage** - Estimate fit (e.g., "90% Role Match", "85% Role Match")

## Example Mapping

If job requires "Java microservices":
✅ **Strong** | Java Spring experience at UKG, Cardlytics; built microservices for 200M MAU

If job requires "Cassandra":
✅ **Relevant** | Distributed systems expertise; built event-driven architecture for 70M users (note: specific Cassandra experience limited)

If job requires "Healthcare domain":
✅ **Relevant** | Patientco (Waystar): HIPAA-compliant healthcare payment processing

## Output Style
- Use professional but direct language
- Focus on concrete examples over general claims
- Organize information for quick scanning during interviews
- Include company names and specific technologies
- Show progression of experience and expertise
